Event Overview - Project Expo

Project Expo is a technical event in which the participants should prepare their project at home and bring the project to demonstrate before the judges.

There is only one round, and the best
project is chosen by the judges,
with respective prizes given away.

Event Rules

General Rules and Guidelines

  • Each team can have a maximum of 4 members.
  • The competition is open to only undergraduate students.
  • Members should have a valid student ID card from their college.
  • A team member can participate in only one project presentation.

Regulations for Competition and Display of Project

  • The project for competition must be displayed and presented with an effective PowerPoint presentation.
  • The presentation should contain a simple description of their project.
  • The candidates can take help from the following points to describe their project in the PPT:
    • Introduction
    • Block diagram
    • Working principle
    • Advantages and disadvantages
    • Future work
  • Participants must exhibit their project on Engineers Day, September 16th November, 2024.
  • Teams will be given a space to set up their model/design at the venue, Center-3, for exhibition and judging.

Important Note

  • Partial outputs are considered.
  • All selected participants will receive a participation certificate.
  • Basic system requirements will be made available to participants as per the project requirements.
  • Participants should bring all their requirements to execute their project.
  • The decision of the judges shall be treated as final and binding.
  • The panel of judges consists of subject experts from our college.
  • The organizers reserve the right to change any or all of the above rules as they deem fit.

Judging Criteria

  • Innovation
  • Design (Compactness and efficiency of work done)
  • Efficiency (Performance of the task)
  • Relevance to practical needs
