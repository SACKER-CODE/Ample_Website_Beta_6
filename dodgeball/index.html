Event Overview

In Dodge Ball, two teams, typically of 6 players each, compete to eliminate the opposing team. Players form a large circle with two players in the middle, aiming to hit those in the circle with a ball to eliminate them. The game continues until all players on one team are eliminated or a set time limit is reached. The team with more players remaining wins.

  • Re-entry Fee: Rs 100 per team
  • Team Composition:Each team contains 6 members.

Gameplay

In Dodgeball, players aim to dodge the ball thrown by the opposing team. If hit, they are out. The game continues until all players on one team are eliminated or a set time limit is reached. The team with remaining players at the end wins.
