Circuit Haunting

The event aims to assess participants' knowledge and skills in circuit designing, circuit solving, and circuit identification across three levels:

Event Summary:

  • Level 1: Focuses on connecting the components shown to the participants in a directed method.
  • Level 2: Involves testing the knowledge of students on electric circuits, electronic components, and logical design.
  • Level 3: Challenges participants to implement circuits with the given components on a breadboard.

How the Event is Conducted

Level 1: Circuit Basics

  • Present participants with tasks involving connecting the electrical components and logical gates.
  • Judges assess participants' accuracy and ability to design the logical and electrical circuits.
  • Simple prelims round filtering participants for the next level.
  • Google form link will be provided for the first round.

Level 2: Circuit Solving

  • Participants receive problems requiring them to solve Boolean expressions and electrical circuits.
  • Judges evaluate participants' logical reasoning and ability to solve circuits based on given input-output requirements.
  • Question papers will be evaluated manually by the faculty.

Level 3: Circuit Implementation and Design (Final Round)

  • Participants design circuits on a breadboard with given components.
  • Judges assess participants' problem-solving skills, creativity, and understanding of circuit functionality.
  • Results will be declared after evaluation.
