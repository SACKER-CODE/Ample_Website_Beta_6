Gully Cricket

Gully Cricket is a fun and informal version of cricket
played in streets and open spaces.
The registration fee is Rs. 250 per team,
with a re-entry fee of Rs. 150 per team.
Each team consists of 5 members.

Event Rules

Rules

  • Gully cricket emphasizes fair play and sportsmanship. Players are expected to abide by the rules, respect their opponents, and play in the spirit of the game.
  • There can be variations in the rules regarding boundaries, such as hitting the ball over a certain height or distance being declared as six, or the ball bouncing off a wall counting as runs.
  • Depending on the locality and the preferences of the players, additional rules or modifications to existing rules may be introduced to suit the conditions or enhance the enjoyment of the game.
