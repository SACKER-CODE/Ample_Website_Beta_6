Event Rules

Screening Round

  • Each team would be given a set of 15 objective type questions.
  • Time limit: 15 minutes.
  • Only qualified teams will proceed to the STAGE ROUNDS.
  • In case of a tie, 3 extra questions will be asked for final selection.

Stage Rounds

First Round (Lap Round)

  • Each team will be asked 5 objective questions.
  • Marks: 10 marks for correct answers, no negative marking.
  • Questions forwarded to the next team if unanswered (No extra time).
  • Teams selected: Top 6 teams move to the 2nd round.
  • Answering time: 30 seconds per question.

Second Round (Audio-visual Round)

  • This round consists of audio-visual questions.
  • Marks: 10 marks for correct answers, no negative marking.
  • Each team will be asked 2 audio-visual questions.
  • Audio or video will be played for 60 seconds, answering time is 30 seconds.
  • Questions forwarded to the next team if unanswered.
  • This round is not an elimination round.

Third Round (Picture Identify Round)

  • Identify the object in the picture.
  • Marks: 10 marks for correct answers, -5 marks for wrong/no answer.
  • Answering time: 30 seconds per question.
  • Top 3 teams will move to the final round.

Final Round (Rapid Fire Round)

  • Each team will answer 5 rapid-fire questions within 50 seconds.
  • Marks: 10 marks for correct answers, -5 marks for wrong/no answer.
  • Question change time: 10 seconds per question.
  • Winners: Top 2 teams will be selected as Winners and Runners.
