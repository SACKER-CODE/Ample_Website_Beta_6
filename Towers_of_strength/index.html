Tower of Strength

This event introduces participants to real-time robots
through two levels of interactive learning.

In Level 1, participants take a quiz on basic robotics knowledge .

In Level 2, participants robot components and engage in a JAM session on advanced robots.
Notable robots like Atlas, Sophia, and ASIMO will be featured in discussions

Registration Fee : 100/-

Event Overview

  • Level 1 : The event is structured as a representation of real-time robots and aims to cover basic information about robots in two levels.
  • Level 2 : Participants are asked basic questions about robots to qualify for the next level. They will be asked simple questions in the form of a quiz.
