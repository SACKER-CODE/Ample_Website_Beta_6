BUILD & BREAK THE PYRAMID

This event involves two participants:
Participant 1 will build a pyramid while Participant 2 will break it.
After completion of each cycle,
the team will be awarded 1 point.

Participation is in teams of 2 members,
and the entry fee is Rs. 50/-.

  • Time duration for each team: 180 seconds

Event Rules

Rules

  • Participants should not exchange their positions during the event.
  • Points will be awarded only after the completion of each cycle.
  • The team scoring the maximum points will be considered the winner.
