Welcome to the heart of Tech-Spark

This is S V C E ......

SV College of Engineering is a prestigious institution set amidst lush greenery and rolling hills, providing an inspiring and serene environment for students and staff alike. The campus, characterized by its manicured lawns and abundant green spaces, creates a tranquil ambiance that enhances the learning experience, harmonizing nature with innovative, eco-friendly infrastructure. Equipped with state-of-the-art facilities, including modern classrooms, well-furnished libraries, and advanced laboratories, the college supports a diverse range of academic disciplines and research. Beyond academics, it prioritizes the holistic development of students through various recreational and sports facilities, fostering an active and balanced lifestyle. The sense of community is palpable, with interactive student spaces and peaceful study areas designed to promote collaboration and creativity.

Campus offers a rich educational experience where students can explore their potential in a vibrant environment. This year, we are excited to introduce Tech Spark, a reimagined event dedicated to technical excellence and innovation. Tech Spark will provide a platform for students to engage with the latest advancements in technology through coding challenges, hands-on workshops, and interactive seminars. It aims to ignite curiosity and collaboration among budding engineers, developers, and tech enthusiasts. This evolution follows the journey from our original technical event, Ample, which later merged with Josh to form Spark—a blend of technical and non-technical competitions. While last year's Spark celebrated innovation and creativity, this year’s focus on Tech Spark emphasizes our commitment to nurturing technical expertise and fostering innovation in response to the rapidly changing technological landscape.

Dr N. Sudhakar Reddy

Principal, SVCE
