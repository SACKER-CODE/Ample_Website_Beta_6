Event Rules

  • Theme Adherence: Submitted photos must align with the exhibition's theme.
  • Ownership and Rights: Photographers must own the rights to their images and secure consent from subjects.
  • Content Restrictions: No images with violent, hateful, or explicit content unless clearly for artistic purposes.
  • Selection Process: Photos will be chosen by a panel or curator based on originality, technique, and relevance.
  • Display Guidelines: Ensure that all prints meet size, framing, and resolution requirements for display.
