Movie Quiz

Movie Quiz is a game
where there will be two participants per team.
A movie clip will be shown to the team,
and questions will be asked based on the movie clip.
The team must answer the
questions within the given time limit.

The entry fee for this event is Rs. 50/- (for one team).

Event Rules

Rules

  • The game is played as a team (2 participants per team).
  • The number of correct answers out of 10 questions answered by the team will be noted.
  • The team that answers the most correct answers out of 10 questions will be declared the winner, and a cash prize will be awarded to that team.
