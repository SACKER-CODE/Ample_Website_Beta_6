Event Overview

Capture The Flag (CTF) Hackathon organized by the CIS Association,
in collaboration with IIT Tirupati.
This event is designed for cybersecurity enthusiasts to showcase their skills in solving real-world challenges.
The hackathon will consist of three rounds with progressively tougher challenges,
and it offers both individual and team participation options.

  • Entry Fee: Rs 250 per indivisual participant.
  • Entry Fee: Rs 800 for a team of 4 members. (200 per participant)
  • Every participant will receive a certificate of participation from IIT Tirupati
  • Prize Pool: 5000

Event Rules

Round Descriptions

  • Round 1 : Participants will face a series of steganography challenges where they must uncover hidden data embedded within images or other file types.

  • Round 2 : This round will involve analyzing network traffic. Participants will need to track packets and extract hidden clues from the provided packet data to progress.

  • Round 3 : Participants will be tasked with identifying bugs and vulnerabilities in a given website. Successful exploitation will reveal the final answer.
