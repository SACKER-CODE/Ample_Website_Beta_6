Poster Presentation

The Poster Presentation Event is an individual competition designed to enhance
participants' skills in creating and presenting technical posters.
Open to all B.E/B.Tech students, participants are required to submit posters
on department-specific topics such as Cloud Computing,
Signal Processing, Mechanical Engineering Trends, and more.
The event focuses on broadening students' knowledge in their field of study,
with participants expected to present their research findings
before a panel of judges.
The competition also emphasizes effective communication skills, critical thinking, and technical expertise in their chosen topic.

Rules

Purpose:

To enhance participants' ability in writing or making papers in English as well as presenting them, and to broaden the minds of the participants.

Team Formation:

  • 1. Team can have a maximum of 2 participants from B.Tech/B.E/Diploma.
  • 2. No participant can be part of more than one team.
  • 3. Participants forming a team do not have to be from the same college.
  • 4. There is no restriction on the number of teams from the same college.

Presentation Rules:

  • 1. The paper submitted must be presented during the event.
  • 2. Hard copies of the paper should be submitted to the judges before the presentation.
  • 3. Teams will have 8 minutes to present their paper, followed by 2 minutes for questions from the judges.
  • 4. Participants must present their papers in MS-PowerPoint (ppt) format only.
  • 5. Violating any rule may result in disqualification of the paper.
  • 6. Organizers reserve the right to change/update the rules at any time, and will try to inform participants. However, it is the participants' responsibility to stay updated.

Paper Format Rules:

  • The paper must follow IEEE format.
  • 1. Abstract may not exceed two pages.
  • 2. The front page should display the paper's title at the top and the team members' details at the bottom (as mentioned in the abstract).
  • 3. The paper must include an abstract.
  • 4. Font style: Times New Roman.
  • 5. Font size for text: 12 points.
  • 6. Font size for headings: 16 points.
  • 7. Font size for sub-headings: 14 points.
  • 8. Use a margin of 1 inch on all sides.
