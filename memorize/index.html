MEMORIZE

This event consists of two types of questions: Display and Audio based. After completion of the question, participants should write the order of displayed things on paper and also do the same for audio-based questions within the given time.

Participation is individual, and the entry fee is Rs. 30/-.

  • Total questions: 20
  • Total time given for each question and answer: 60 seconds

Rules

  • Questions will be asked only once. No repetition of questions.
  • Every answer should be written on paper.
  • Answers will be considered only if they follow the correct order.
  • For each correct answer, 10 points will be awarded.
  • The participant with the maximum points scored will be considered the winner.
