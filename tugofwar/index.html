Tug of War

Tug of War is a competitive game
where teams pull against each other on a rope.
The registration fee is Rs. 150 per team,
with a re-entry fee of Rs. 100 per team.
Each team consists of 5 members.

  • Registration fee: 150 per team
  • Re-entry feer: 100 per team

Event Rules

Rules

  • A level playing surface, free from obstacles, with the center line marked clearly. It's often on grass or another soft surface to minimize injuries.
  • Teams line up evenly on either side of the center line, with each member gripping the rope firmly. A neutral official signals the start of the match, often with a whistle or command.
  • Players pull the rope, trying to drag the opposing team across the center line or into their own designated winning zone.
  • The team that successfully pulls their opponents across the center line or into their winning zone is declared the winner.
